sort1 uses: Bubble Sort

How do you know?: Sorted numbers run much faster than random and reversed numbers

sort2 uses: Merge Sort

How do you know?: All three cases (sorted, random, and reversed numbers) are sorted in about the same time, similar to bubble sort's best time

sort3 uses: Selection Sort

How do you know?: All three cases (sorted, random, and reversed numbers) take about the same time, but much slower than merge sort
